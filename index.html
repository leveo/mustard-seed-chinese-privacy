🔒 Privacy Policy

Mustard Seed Chinese ("the App") is developed by Leo Song. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of all users, including children.

👤 Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique User ID and, optionally, your email address (used only for account recovery). We do not receive your name or other personal details unless you choose to share them.

Learning Progress: Lesson completion, quiz scores, Bible highlights, notes, and check-in streaks are stored to track your child's learning journey.

Preferences: App settings such as language preference, font size, and notification choices.

📱 Local Data Storage

All progress and preferences are stored on your device first. Children can learn even without an internet connection.

☁️ Backend Sync

When backend mode is on (the default), progress, Bible highlights, and notes sync securely to our servers for multi-device access. All data is transmitted via encrypted HTTPS using a union-merge strategy so no data is ever lost. You can turn this off anytime in Settings.

✓ Only progress, highlights, notes, and check-in data is synced — never voice data

🎤 Microphone & Speech Recognition

Read-along and memorization features use on-device speech recognition (Apple Speech Framework) to evaluate pronunciation. All voice processing happens entirely on the device.

✓ Voice data never leaves your device

🔌 Offline Mode

If you prefer no data sent to servers, enable Offline Mode in Settings. Everything runs locally with no network communication.

👁‍🗨 No Tracking, No Ads

We use no third-party trackers, collect no analytics, and show no ads. This app is designed for learning, not data collection.

👶 Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Mustard Seed Chinese is designed for use by children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not track children's activity across apps or websites.
  • We do not show advertisements of any kind.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not collect more information than is necessary for the app to function.
  • Voice recordings are processed entirely on the device and are never stored or transmitted.
  • Parents can review and delete all data at any time through Parental Controls.

🗑 Data Deletion

You can delete all server data anytime through Parental Controls inside the app. Local data can be cleared by deleting the app from your device. If you need help, contact us at spiritualgrowthgarden@gmail.com.

📣 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

👍 In Summary

We collect only essential learning data, with no tracking, no ads, and no voice uploads. You have full control of your data.

🔒 隐私政策

芥菜种中文（以下简称"本应用"）由 Leo Song 开发。本隐私政策描述了我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。我们致力于保护所有用户（包括儿童）的隐私。

👤 我们收集的信息

账户信息：当您通过 Apple 登录时，我们会收到一个唯一的用户 ID，以及您可选提供的电子邮箱（仅用于账户恢复）。除非您主动分享，否则我们不会获取您的姓名或其他个人信息。

学习进度：课程完成情况、测验成绩、圣经高亮、笔记和打卡记录，用于追踪孩子的学习历程。

偏好设置：语言偏好、字体大小、通知设置等应用设置。

📱 本地数据存储

所有学习进度和偏好设置优先存储在您的设备上。即使没有网络连接，孩子也可以正常学习和练习。

☁️ 后台同步

开启后台模式时（默认开启），学习进度、圣经高亮和笔记会安全地同步到我们的服务器，以便在多设备间保持一致。所有数据通过加密的 HTTPS 传输，使用合并策略确保数据不会丢失。您随时可以在设置中关闭此功能。

✓ 仅同步学习进度、高亮、笔记和打卡记录，不存储语音

🎤 麦克风与语音识别

跟读和背诵功能使用设备内置的语音识别（Apple Speech Framework）来评估发音。语音处理完全在设备本地完成。

✓ 语音数据不会离开您的设备

🔌 离线模式

如果您不希望任何数据发送到服务器，可以在设置中启用「离线模式」。此模式下，所有功能完全在本地运行，不会有任何网络通信。

👁‍🗨 无追踪，无广告

我们不使用任何第三方追踪工具，不收集分析数据，不显示任何广告。这个应用是为孩子的学习而设计的，不是为了收集数据。

👶 儿童隐私保护（COPPA 合规）

芥菜种中文专为儿童使用而设计，我们高度重视儿童隐私：

  • 我们不会跨应用或网站追踪儿童的活动。
  • 我们不展示任何形式的广告。
  • 我们不与第三方共享任何数据。
  • 我们不收集超出应用运行所需的信息。
  • 语音录音完全在设备上处理，不会被存储或传输。
  • 家长可以随时通过「家长模式」查看和删除所有数据。

🗑 数据删除

您可以随时在「家长模式」中删除服务器上保存的所有学习数据。本地数据可通过删除应用来清除。如需帮助，请联系 spiritualgrowthgarden@gmail.com。
